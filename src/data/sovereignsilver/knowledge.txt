Sovereign Silver is a natural health and wellness brand produced by Natural Immunogenics Corporation.

Natural Immunogenics Corporation was founded in 1998 by Stephen Quinto.

The company was established with a mission centered on health sovereignty, which emphasizes personal responsibility and natural approaches to well-being.

Sovereign Silver focuses on the development, manufacturing, and distribution of bio-active mineral hydrosols.

The flagship product line, Bio-Active Silver Hydrosol, was launched in 1999.

A professional-grade silver hydrosol product, Argentyn-23, was introduced in 2001.

By 2005, Sovereign Silver became the number one selling silver supplement in the United States based on industry sales data.

The company remains family-owned and operates its own state-of-the-art manufacturing and laboratory facilities.

Sovereign Silver invests heavily in research and development to refine hydrosol technology and mineral formulations.

The company maintains affiliations with academic research centers and external laboratories to support scientific validation.

Sovereign Silver’s guiding values include integrity, exceptional quality, continuous improvement, transparency, and regulatory compliance.

All Sovereign Silver products are manufactured under current Good Manufacturing Practices.

The company’s facilities are NSF-registered and audited to GMP standards.

Sovereign Silver products are non-GMO, vegan, gluten-free, and free from common allergens.

The brand emphasizes the use of minimal ingredients, typically consisting only of purified water and elemental minerals.

Bio-Active Silver Hydrosol is a clear, tasteless liquid composed of 99.999 percent pure silver suspended in pharmaceutical-grade water.

The hydrosol contains silver particles at a concentration of approximately 10 parts per million.

The company emphasizes that the silver particles are bio-active and naturally formed rather than engineered nanotechnology.

Sovereign Silver states that more than 98 percent of particles remain bio-available for absorption.

The product is available in multiple formats including dropper bottles, fine mist sprays, and larger twist-top bottles.

A Kids Bio-Active Silver Hydrosol line is available for children aged four and above.

Children’s formulations follow weight-based dosing guidelines.

Sovereign Silver also offers a Natural Nasal Spray formulated with silver hydrosol and herbal extracts.

The nasal spray is designed to cleanse nasal passages and support sinus comfort.

Topical products include Sovereign Silver First Aid Gel and Natural Healing Gel.

These gels are colorless, sting-free, and dermatologically tested for skin sensitivity.

Topical products are marketed for minor skin irritations such as cuts, scrapes, burns, bites, rashes, and acne.

Sovereign Silver also produces a dedicated pet product line for dogs and cats.

Pet products include Pets Bio-Active Silver Hydrosol and Pets First Aid Gel.

The pet line is certified by the National Animal Supplement Council.

Pet formulations are tasteless, odorless, and designed to be safe if licked.

In addition to silver products, Sovereign Silver offers Bio-Active Copper Hydrosol.

The copper hydrosol is formulated with Copper(I) and Copper(II) to support connective tissue, joints, and skin health.

All copper products are produced at low concentrations to remain within daily intake guidelines.

Sovereign Silver emphasizes laboratory verification of particle size, purity, and stability.

Testing methods include transmission electron microscopy and spectroscopy.

The company follows Good Laboratory Practices and ISO-accredited quality systems.

Sovereign Silver positions its products as dietary supplements and topical wellness products.

The company explicitly states that its products are not drugs.

All product labels include FDA-required disclaimers regarding evaluation and intended use.

Sovereign Silver states that its products support immune function rather than treat or cure disease.

The company highlights silver as a naturally occurring trace mineral found in food and water.

Safety messaging emphasizes low concentration, purity, and long-term use within established reference doses.

Sovereign Silver states that products are safe for frequent use when taken as directed.

The company provides extensive consumer education through FAQs, labeling, and usage guidelines.

Products are sold directly through the company website and through health food stores and select pharmacies.

Sovereign Silver also offers bulk, practitioner, and retail distribution programs.

The brand emphasizes transparency, science-backed quality, and long-term trust with consumers.

What Sovereign Silver does NOT do:
- It does not manufacture pharmaceutical drugs.
- It does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.
- It does not replace professional medical care.
- It does not offer synthetic or engineered nanomaterials.

OPTIMA BATTERIES – FAQ
=====================

APPEND INSTRUCTIONS
-------------------
This FAQ may be appended to existing OPTIMA FAQs.
Questions are atomic and independent.

--------------------------------------------------

Q1. What is OPTIMA Batteries?
OPTIMA Batteries is a battery manufacturer known for AGM and lithium battery technology.

Q2. When was OPTIMA Batteries founded?
OPTIMA Batteries was founded in 1972.

Q3. What is SpiralCell technology?
SpiralCell technology uses tightly wound pure lead plates in AGM construction.

Q4. Are OPTIMA batteries maintenance-free?
Yes, OPTIMA batteries require no water refilling or routine maintenance.

Q5. What type of batteries does OPTIMA manufacture?
OPTIMA manufactures AGM lead-acid batteries and lithium (LiFePO₄) batteries.

Q6. What is the difference between RedTop and YellowTop?
RedTop is designed for engine starting, while YellowTop supports deep-cycle use.

Q7. What applications are YellowTop batteries used for?
They are used in vehicles with high electrical loads and repeated cycling.

Q8. What are BlueTop batteries used for?
BlueTop batteries are used in marine, RV, and off-road applications.

Q9. Are BlueTop batteries deep-cycle?
Some BlueTop models are dual-purpose and support deep-cycle usage.

Q10. What is OrangeTop?
OrangeTop is OPTIMA’s lithium battery line for lightweight, high-crank applications.

Q11. Can OPTIMA batteries be mounted sideways?
Yes, AGM OPTIMA batteries can be mounted in multiple orientations.

Q12. Do OPTIMA batteries leak acid?
No, they are sealed and spill-proof.

Q13. What charging voltage is recommended?
Charging voltage should typically be between 13.8V and 15.0V.

Q14. Can OPTIMA batteries be overcharged?
Yes, overcharging above recommended voltage can cause damage.

Q15. How long can an OPTIMA battery sit in storage?
A fully charged battery can last 8–12 months in storage.

Q16. Do OPTIMA batteries emit gas?
They do not emit gas under normal charging conditions.

Q17. What company owns OPTIMA Batteries?
OPTIMA is a brand under Clarios.

Q18. Are OPTIMA batteries recyclable?
Yes, they are recyclable through certified facilities.

Q19. Is OPTIMA a gel battery?
No, OPTIMA batteries are AGM, not gel.

Q20. Where can OPTIMA batteries be purchased?
They are sold through authorized automotive, marine, and battery retailers.

END OF FAQ
